Trays for Surface-Mount Components on high-volume PCBA's

- For this project at Celestica, custom trays were designed and 3D-printed using PETG (polyethylene terephthalate glycol) filament material on a Prusa MK3S 3D-printer. These trays were used for high-volume production of PCBA's with surface mount or through-hole mount components. These trays were utilized to hold the components in place and allow the SMT machines to solder through reflow and/or wave soldering processes. Within this project, there was a heavy focus on the tolerancing and fitment of these components, as they could be damaged very easily during transportation and production.
